This is a precision fender-style electric bass designed by me!

It's got three knobs for volume, treble and bass, as well as a custom PCB for active sound managment, and a dual amp system to play audio to EITHER a 1/4" jack OR a standard 3.5mm headphone jack. 
It also has a small rechargable battery to let me play with my headphones away from an amp or power source for hopefully a few hours at a time. 

The bass is pretty standard, and if you wanna 3d print the two parts, buy all the components, the wiring diagram should get you through the complex part!

I made this as I don't currently have an electric bass to play on, and would really like the chance to make my own instrument and start playing new types of music!
